Lake George Brief History

Lake George was named by William Johnson in 1755 for his King, George II of England. Lake George was the battlegrounds for the 7 year war, The French and Indian War. Today Lake George is a small tourist town.

Areas to Visit

  • Rogers Rock for cliff jumping
  • Prospect Mountain hiking trail
  • Fort William Henry
  • Shelving Rock Falls
  • Lake George Fireworks
  • Winter Carnival
